WHY U.S. TOUR

After the Taiwanese film, Cape No. 7, successfully swept audiences off their feet in 2008 and set the highest box office, the director, Wei Te-Sheng, with his newest film, 52Hz, I Love You, will be in North America this year.

WHY THE MOVIE

“52Hz” refers to a very unusual frequency sung by the loneliest whale in the Pacific Ocean. The mysterious pitch is like no other; it sounds like the whale is crying out for companionship that never comes…

However, this Taiwanese film, 52Hz, I Love You, captures both the loneliness and warmth in a metropolitan city--Taipei City, and suggests that even in the hustle and bustle of metropolis, millions of lonely people crying out for LOVE.

WHAT'S MORE

Remarkably, the movie hits another record on “the first Taiwanese musical film,” and again, the director found new appealing actors to present a series of romantically comedic stories with beautiful original songs. It is a story that take place in modern Taipei City, Taiwan, on Valentine’s Day. It contrasts crowd and loneliness, love and being loved. It will bring a brand-new impression about the city and Taiwan to you.

On the way home from the screen, you might find yourself humming those bittersweet songs as those characters do with happiness.

FAQ:
1. 我是TWSA會員，我有繳會費，為什麼我不能免費入場？
這次活動是由OSU的一群熱血博士生，自發為Columbus的大家爭取這個機會，並肩負起所有工作。TWSA只是協辦的角色，並非主辦方；而且為保障觀影品質，主辦單位劃定了最佳觀影區域，座位有限。所以TWSA身為最大協辦單位，也只能盡力爭取到50席；礙於空間與各方考量，這次特映會只能招待50位會員免費入場，還請沒被抽中的會員見諒。

2. 什麼時候會知道我有沒有中獎？
主辦單位將在10/25/2016前，依照google表單上所填寫的email，寄發確認信。特映會當天請您印出確認信，入場時，憑email於特映會會場換取入場券入場。

3. 如果沒被抽中，我該怎麼辦？
首先，僅代表主辦單位，感謝您對特映會的支持！如果真的沒被抽中，真的非常不好意思，但如果您還是願意出席特映會，就要麻煩您直接買票進場看電影了。抽獎時間會視售票情況彈性調整，但您若不想經歷抽獎的期待與風險，也可以直接買票喔！
